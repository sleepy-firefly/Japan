**Senso-ji (Asakusa, Tokyo)**

Senso-ji is Tokyo's oldest major Buddhist temple and one of the most visited cultural sites in Japan.

The Kaminarimon Gate and Nakamise approach street make it one of the best places to experience traditional urban temple atmosphere.

&emsp;&emsp;**Best season/month**

- March-April for spring walks around Asakusa.
- November for milder weather and evening visits.

&emsp;&emsp;**Practical note**

- Visit early morning for lighter crowds and cleaner photo conditions.
