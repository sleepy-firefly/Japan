**Hase-dera (Kamakura)**

Hase-dera is one of Kamakura's best-known temples, often visited together with the Great Buddha route.

It is especially notable in early summer hydrangea season, when Kamakura temple hillsides become one of the top seasonal flower destinations near Tokyo.

<img src="../images/hase_dera_kamakura.jpg" alt="Hase-dera temple in Kamakura" style="width: 80%; height: auto;" />

&emsp;&emsp;**Best season/month**

- June for hydrangeas.
- November for cooler temple walks.

&emsp;&emsp;**Practical note**

- Combine with Kotoku-in and Komachi Street in one day.
