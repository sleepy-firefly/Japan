**Sanjusangendo (Kyoto)**

Sanjusangendo is one of Kyoto's most remarkable temple halls, known for its long wooden architecture and historic Buddhist statuary context.

It is easy to add to a Higashiyama-focused day with Kiyomizu-dera and nearby historic neighborhoods.

&emsp;&emsp;**Best season/month**

- Spring and autumn for comfortable Kyoto walking days.

&emsp;&emsp;**Practical note**

- Pair with morning temple visits and late-afternoon old-street walks.
