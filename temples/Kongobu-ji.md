**Kongobu-ji (Koyasan, Wakayama)**

Kongobu-ji is one of Koyasan's most important temple complexes and a central stop on the sacred mountain route.

It is usually visited together with Danjo Garan and Okunoin as part of a temple-stay itinerary.

&emsp;&emsp;**Best season/month**

- Spring and autumn for cooler walking weather.
- Winter for quieter temple-stay atmosphere.

&emsp;&emsp;**Practical note**

- Reserve temple lodging (shukubo) early in foliage season.
