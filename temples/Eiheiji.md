**Eiheiji (Fukui)**

Eiheiji is one of Japan's most important Zen training temples, known for cedar-forest surroundings and a strong monastic atmosphere.

It is one of the best temple-focused day trips in Fukui Prefecture and a key stop for travelers interested in Zen heritage.

&emsp;&emsp;**Best season/month**

- Spring and autumn for comfortable temple grounds walking.

&emsp;&emsp;**Practical note**

- Visit on weekday mornings for a quieter atmosphere.
