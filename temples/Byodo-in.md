**Byodo-in (Uji, Kyoto Prefecture)**

Byodo-in is one of Japan's most famous Pure Land temples and a key cultural highlight in Uji.

It combines well with tea-town walks and riverside routes, making Uji one of the easiest half-day temple trips from Kyoto.

<img src="../images/byodo_in.jpg" alt="Byodo-in temple in Uji" style="width: 80%; height: auto;" />

&emsp;&emsp;**Best season/month**

- Spring and autumn for temple and tea-town walks.

&emsp;&emsp;**Practical note**

- Best paired with Ujigami Shrine and tea shops in one loop.
