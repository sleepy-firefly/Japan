**November (11月)**

November is one of the most beautiful months in Japan because autumn colors reach many famous gardens and temple areas; see the [Temple Index](../temples/Temple%20Index.md) for top options. The weather is usually cool and pleasant, making it one of the easiest months for sightseeing.

Some highlights of traveling in Japan in November include:

* Maple leaves and ginkgo trees are at their best in many cities, especially around temple grounds and traditional gardens; see the [Temple Index](../temples/Temple%20Index.md).

* November is a very comfortable month for walking-focused trips, with crisp air, low humidity, and many sunny days.

* This is also a good time to combine city travel with countryside or hot spring stays, especially in mountain areas with autumn scenery.

* Popular autumn foliage spots can still be very busy, so visiting early in the morning often makes the experience much more peaceful.
