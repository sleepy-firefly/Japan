**December (12月)**

December marks the start of winter in Japan. In many places the air is cold but clear, and the month has a festive atmosphere thanks to illuminations, year-end markets, and the approach of the New Year holiday.

Some highlights of traveling in Japan in December include:

* Winter illuminations decorate many cities and shopping districts, making evening walks especially enjoyable.

* Ski season begins in many mountain areas and in Hokkaido, so December can be a good time for early winter trips.

* Hot springs become especially appealing as temperatures drop, and ryokan stays feel particularly cozy at this time of year.

* Toward the end of the month, Japan becomes busy with year-end travel and New Year preparations, so trains and hotels can fill up quickly.

* For otaku culture trips in Tokyo, December can include the winter edition of [Comiket](../events/otaku/Comiket.md).
