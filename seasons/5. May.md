**May (5月)**

May is one of the best months for travel in Japan. The weather is usually warm but not yet humid, skies are often clear, and the landscapes are full of fresh green leaves after spring.

Some highlights of traveling in Japan in May include:

* Golden Week falls at the beginning of May and is one of the busiest holiday periods in Japan, so trains, hotels, and famous attractions can become very crowded.

* Outside of Golden Week, May is an excellent month for hiking and day trips; for major temple picks see the [Temple Index](../temples/Temple%20Index.md).

* Mountains, parks, and rural areas look especially beautiful with fresh spring greenery, making it a great time for scenic train rides and countryside travel.

* Late spring flowers such as wisteria, azaleas, and roses are also in season in many parts of the country.
