**April (4月)**

April is one of the most popular months to visit Japan because the weather is mild and spring is in full swing. Across much of the country, cherry blossoms, fresh greenery, and comfortable temperatures make it one of the most beautiful times of year.

Some highlights of traveling in Japan in April include:

* Cherry blossom season reaches many major cities in April, and parks, riversides, and castle grounds become some of the most scenic places in the country.

* In Aomori, the [Sakura Yabusame Festival](../events/festivals/Sakura%20Yabusame%20Festival.md) adds a more unusual spring atmosphere to the blossom season.

* April is a great month for walking around historic districts and gardens; for top temple choices see the [Temple Index](../temples/Temple%20Index.md).

* Because it is such a popular travel season, famous blossom spots and weekend trains can be crowded, so it is a good month to book accommodation early.
