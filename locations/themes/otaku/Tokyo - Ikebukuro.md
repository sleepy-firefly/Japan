**Tokyo - Ikebukuro**

Ikebukuro is a core anime and character-goods area, especially strong for shop density and fandom-focused floor guides.

&emsp;**Best For**

- Character goods and collab cafe hunting
- Large hobby chains in one walkable zone
- Mixed anime and game shopping without Akihabara crowds

&emsp;**Suggested Half-Day Route**

- East exit flagship hobby stores
- Sunshine City complex and event spaces
- Evening cafe or themed dinner stop

&emsp;**Practical Notes**

- Easy access from JR Yamanote Line
- Sunshine City can take longer than expected; plan at least 2 to 3 hours
- Weekday afternoons are usually easier for browsing

&emsp;**City-to-City Routing**

- Best inbound stop: from Akihabara or Ueno via JR Yamanote Line
- Next same-day otaku stop: Nakano Broadway (via Shinjuku transfer)
- Long-distance continuation: Tokyo Station for Shinkansen to Nagoya/Kansai

&emsp;**Budget Guidance**

- Tight: prioritize free-entry shops and skip premium collab cafes
- Medium: include one collab cafe and one planned merch purchase
- Relaxed: add character events, multiple cafes, and larger goods haul
