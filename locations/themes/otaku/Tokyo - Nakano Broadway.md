**Tokyo - Nakano Broadway**

Nakano Broadway is a legendary destination for vintage manga, rare figures, old merch lines, and specialist second-hand stores.

&emsp;**Best For**

- Rare and older collectible categories
- Niche franchise goods
- Smaller specialist stores in one building

&emsp;**Suggested Half-Day Route**

- Nakano Sun Mall approach
- Full floor-by-floor browse in Nakano Broadway
- Coffee break and second pass for targeted purchases

&emsp;**Practical Notes**

- Budget range can vary widely by rarity
- Bring a short target list to avoid impulse overspend
- Item condition grading is usually reliable, but check labels closely

&emsp;**City-to-City Routing**

- Best inbound stop: Shinjuku to Nakano (short JR hop)
- Next same-day otaku stop: Ikebukuro or Akihabara depending on priorities
- Best onward city transfer: Shinkansen from Tokyo Station for Nagoya/Osaka

&emsp;**Budget Guidance**

- Tight: prioritize browsing and low-price bins; avoid rare-item floors
- Medium: plan one collectible category and one backup option
- Relaxed: target rare/archival items with extra time for condition checks
