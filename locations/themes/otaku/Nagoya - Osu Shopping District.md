**Nagoya - Osu Shopping District**

Osu is Nagoya's mixed culture district where anime, games, street food, and second-hand shops overlap in a compact area.

&emsp;**Best For**

- Mid-size otaku shopping circuits
- Mixed-interest groups (shopping plus food)
- Lower-pressure browsing than major Tokyo hubs

&emsp;**Suggested Half-Day Route**

- Osu Kannon area entry
- Core shopping arcades and side streets
- Break for local snacks and cafes

&emsp;**Practical Notes**

- Good option for travelers passing between Tokyo and Kansai
- Some stores are small and easy to miss; walk side alleys too
- Great rainy-day fallback when nearby nature plans are canceled

&emsp;**City-to-City Routing**

- Best inbound stop: Nagoya Station to Osu area by subway
- Ideal sequence use: between Tokyo and Osaka/Kyoto on Shinkansen routes
- Best onward city transfer: continue west to Osaka/Kyoto or east to Tokyo

&emsp;**Budget Guidance**

- Tight: focus on window shopping and low-cost snack stops
- Medium: add one cafe stop and one targeted merch purchase
- Relaxed: multiple specialty purchases and sit-down dining breaks
