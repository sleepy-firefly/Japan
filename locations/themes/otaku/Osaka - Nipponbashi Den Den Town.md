**Osaka - Nipponbashi Den Den Town**

Nipponbashi (Den Den Town) is Osaka's core otaku and electronics area, often compared to Akihabara at a smaller scale.

&emsp;**Best For**

- Figure and model shopping in Kansai
- Arcade and hobby chain browsing
- Combining otaku shopping with food-heavy Osaka plans

&emsp;**Suggested Half-Day Route**

- Start near Namba side access
- Main Den Den Town shopping strip
- Maid cafe or themed cafe stop
- Evening food run in Namba or Dotonbori

&emsp;**Practical Notes**

- Peak crowd times are afternoons on weekends
- Many stores open later in the morning
- Prices can differ from Tokyo by item type, so compare if you are buying high-value items

&emsp;**City-to-City Routing**

- Best inbound stop: Shin-Osaka to Namba area by Osaka Metro/JR
- Next same-day otaku stop: Umeda hobby stores or central Namba arcades
- Best onward city transfer: short rail hop to Kyoto, or Shinkansen toward Nagoya/Tokyo

&emsp;**Budget Guidance**

- Tight: prioritize second-hand chains and fixed-price meal spots
- Medium: one themed cafe plus planned figure/model purchase
- Relaxed: premium collectibles, multiple cafes, and nightlife add-ons
