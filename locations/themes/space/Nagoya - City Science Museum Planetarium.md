**Nagoya - City Science Museum Planetarium**

Nagoya's science museum is one of Japan's strongest astronomy stops, known for its large planetarium dome.

&emsp;**Best For**

- Astronomy-focused day plans between Tokyo and Kansai
- Families or mixed-interest groups
- Indoor science travel in any season

&emsp;**Suggested Half-Day Route**

- Morning museum galleries
- Midday planetarium session
- Late lunch and nearby city walk

&emsp;**Practical Notes**

- Planetarium sessions can fill quickly on weekends
- Arrive early when targeting specific showtimes
- Keep a backup slot in case same-day tickets are limited

&emsp;**City-to-City Routing**

- Ideal position: midway stop between Tokyo and Osaka/Kyoto
- Best inbound: Shinkansen to Nagoya, then local subway
- Best onward continuation: west to Kansai or east to Tokyo

&emsp;**Budget Guidance**

- Tight: museum entry and one fixed show session
- Medium: premium session times plus local cafe breaks
- Relaxed: upgraded transport windows and expanded city add-ons
