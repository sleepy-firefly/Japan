**Tokyo - Miraikan and TeNQ**

Tokyo offers the easiest high-density space-themed day through Odaiba's Miraikan and central-city TeNQ exhibits.

&emsp;**Best For**

- Short-stay travelers who still want a space focus
- Indoor science and astronomy experiences
- Flexible half-day or full-day planning

&emsp;**Suggested Half-Day Route**

- Morning Miraikan session in Odaiba
- Afternoon transfer to TeNQ area
- Evening city plan near Tokyo Dome City

&emsp;**Practical Notes**

- Exhibition schedules can change; check both sites before planning
- Time slots may sell out on weekends/holidays
- Combine with nearby indoor alternatives in bad weather

&emsp;**City-to-City Routing**

- Best inbound base: any central Tokyo hub on JR/Metro lines
- Next same-day extension: Odaiba waterfront or Ueno museum cluster
- Best onward continuation: Tsukuba day trip or Shinkansen toward Nagoya/Osaka

&emsp;**Budget Guidance**

- Tight: select one venue and use standard transit passes
- Medium: do both venues with reserved entry where available
- Relaxed: premium timed slots, add-on attractions, and central stay
