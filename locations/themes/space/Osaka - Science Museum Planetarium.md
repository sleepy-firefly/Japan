**Osaka - Science Museum Planetarium**

Osaka Science Museum provides a practical Kansai astronomy stop with planetarium programs and science exhibits near central Osaka.

&emsp;**Best For**

- Kansai-based space and astronomy planning
- Short evening-capable museum plans
- Rainy-day alternatives in Osaka itineraries

&emsp;**Suggested Half-Day Route**

- Osaka Science Museum entry
- Planetarium session block
- Nakanoshima riverside walk

&emsp;**Practical Notes**

- Session language and schedule may vary by day
- Confirm ticket windows before arrival
- Combine with nearby city museums for a full indoor day

&emsp;**City-to-City Routing**

- Best inbound base: Umeda/Namba with short local transfer
- Same-day extension: central Osaka or Kyoto evening return
- Best onward continuation: Nagoya science stop or Fukuoka by rail/flight

&emsp;**Budget Guidance**

- Tight: one core session and local transit only
- Medium: reserve preferred showtimes and add nearby attractions
- Relaxed: premium stay plus multiple city museum entries
