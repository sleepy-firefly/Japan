**Asahikawa**

Asahikawa is a practical Hokkaido base for winter festivals, city food culture, and trips to Biei/Furano and Daisetsuzan.

&emsp;&emsp;**Top places**

- Asahiyama Zoo
- Hokkaido winter festivals (seasonal)
- Heiwa Dori shopping area and ramen village

&emsp;&emsp;**Logistics**

- Access: JR Limited Express from Sapporo to Asahikawa.
- Typical travel time: 85-95 min.
- Typical one-way fare: JPY 4,500-5,500.

&emsp;&emsp;**Best season**

- February for the winter festivals.
- July-October for nature day trips.
