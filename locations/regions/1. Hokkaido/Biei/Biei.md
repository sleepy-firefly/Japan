**Biei**

Biei is famous for rolling rural landscapes, scenic roads, and iconic blue-water viewpoints.

&emsp;&emsp;**Top places**

- Patchwork Road and Panorama Road
- Shirogane Blue Pond
- Shirahige Falls

&emsp;&emsp;**Logistics**

- Access: JR Furano Line from Asahikawa or Furano.
- Typical travel time: 30-40 min from Asahikawa.
- Typical one-way fare: about JPY 550-900.

&emsp;&emsp;**Best season**

- July-August for flower and field scenery.
- October for autumn colors.
