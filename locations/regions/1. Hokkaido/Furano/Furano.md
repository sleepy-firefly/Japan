**Furano**

Furano is one of Hokkaido's top countryside destinations for flower fields, farm food, and scenic summer routes.

&emsp;&emsp;**Top places**

- Farm Tomita and nearby flower fields
- Furano wine/farm produce areas
- Ningle Terrace forest shopping lane

&emsp;&emsp;**Logistics**

- Access: JR via Asahikawa or Sapporo (transfers common).
- Typical travel time from Sapporo: 2-3 hours.
- Typical one-way fare: JPY 3,000-6,000.

&emsp;&emsp;**Best season**

- Mid July to early August for peak lavender.
- Winter for ski-oriented stays nearby.
