**Sapporo**

Sapporo is Hokkaido's largest city, mixing winter events, food culture, parks, and easy day trips.

&emsp;&emsp;**Suggested order (2 days)**

- Day 1: Odori Park, Clock Tower area, Susukino
- Day 2: Hokkaido Shrine, Nijo Market, evening city view

For full winter event details, see [Hokkaido Winter Festivals](../../../../events/festivals/Hokkaido%20Winter%20Festivals.md).

&emsp;&emsp;**Logistics**

- Access from New Chitose Airport: JR Rapid Airport.
- Typical travel time: 37-40 min.
- Typical one-way fare: JPY 1,150.

&emsp;&emsp;**Best season**

- February for snow festival period.
- June-October for walking and day trips.
