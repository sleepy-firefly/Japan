**Odori (Sapporo District)**

Odori is Sapporo's central park and event zone, known for seasonal festivals and city landmarks.

&emsp;&emsp;**Highlights**

- Odori Park blocks and TV Tower views
- Sapporo Snow Festival sculptures (February)
- Easy walk links to Tanukikoji and Susukino

Event details: [Hokkaido Winter Festivals](../../../../../events/festivals/Hokkaido%20Winter%20Festivals.md)

&emsp;&emsp;**Best season/month**

- February for snow festival.
- Summer evenings for city events and open-air atmosphere.
