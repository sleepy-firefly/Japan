**Daisetsuzan National Park**

Daisetsuzan is Hokkaido's largest national park, known for volcanic landscapes, ropeways, alpine hiking, and early autumn colors.

&emsp;&emsp;**Suggested order (1-2 days)**

- Asahidake Ropeway area and short summit trails
- Sounkyo Gorge walk and waterfalls
- Optional onsen stay around Sounkyo

&emsp;&emsp;**Practical info**

- Access: from Asahikawa by bus to Asahidake or Sounkyo areas.
- Typical bus fares: JPY 1,500-2,500 one way depending on route.
- Ropeway fees are separate and can vary by season.

&emsp;&emsp;**Best season/month**

- July-September for hiking.
- September-October for some of Japan's earliest autumn foliage.
