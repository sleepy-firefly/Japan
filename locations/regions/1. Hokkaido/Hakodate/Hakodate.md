**Hakodate**

Hakodate is a port city known for its morning market, historic slopes, and famous nighttime mountain view.

&emsp;&emsp;**Suggested order (1 day)**

- Morning Market near station
- Motomachi churches and western quarter
- Mt. Hakodate ropeway after sunset

&emsp;&emsp;**Logistics**

- Access from Sapporo: Limited Express Hokuto.
- Typical travel time: 3.5-4 hours.
- Typical one-way fare: JPY 9,000-10,500.

&emsp;&emsp;**Best season**

- May-October for mild weather.
- Winter for atmospheric night views.
