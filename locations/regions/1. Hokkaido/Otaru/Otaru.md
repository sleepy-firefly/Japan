**Otaru**

Otaru is a compact canal town near Sapporo with old warehouses, seafood markets, and nostalgic streets.

&emsp;&emsp;**Suggested order (day trip)**

- Otaru Canal walk
- Sakaimachi shopping street
- Music box and glass craft shops

For full event details, see [Hokkaido Winter Festivals](../../../../events/festivals/Hokkaido%20Winter%20Festivals.md).

&emsp;&emsp;**Logistics**

- Access from Sapporo: JR rapid/local trains.
- Typical travel time: 35-50 min.
- Typical one-way fare: JPY 750-1,000.

&emsp;&emsp;**Best season**

- Summer and autumn for walking.
- February for Snow Light Path event period.
