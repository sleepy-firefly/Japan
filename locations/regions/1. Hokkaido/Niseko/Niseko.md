**Niseko**

Niseko is one of Japan's best-known mountain resort areas for powder snow, onsen, and summer outdoor activities.

&emsp;&emsp;**Top places**

- Grand Hirafu ski area
- Niseko Village slopes/onsen
- Scenic drives and hiking routes in green season

&emsp;&emsp;**Logistics**

- Access from Sapporo/New Chitose: bus or train plus transfer.
- Typical travel time: 2-3.5 hours.
- Typical one-way fare: JPY 2,500-6,500.

&emsp;&emsp;**Best season/month**

- December-February for ski season.
- June-September for hiking and cycling.
