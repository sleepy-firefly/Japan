**Nyuto Onsen**

Nyuto Onsen is a mountain hot spring area with traditional ryokan and scenic forest surroundings.

&emsp;&emsp;**Top places**

- Multiple traditional onsen baths
- Forest walks and local cuisine
- Optional Lake Tazawa side trip

&emsp;&emsp;**Logistics**

- Access: train to Tazawako Station, then bus to Nyuto area.
- Typical one-way budget from Tazawako: JPY 800-1,500.

&emsp;&emsp;**Best season**

- Winter for snow onsen atmosphere.
- Autumn for color scenery.
