**Kakunodate**

Kakunodate is a preserved samurai district known for historic residences and tree-lined streets.

&emsp;&emsp;**Top places**

- Samurai residence district
- Kakunodate cherry avenue
- Small craft and local food stops

&emsp;&emsp;**Logistics**

- Access from Akita/Morioka via JR Akita Shinkansen or local trains.
- Typical fare range: JPY 1,500-6,000 depending on route.

&emsp;&emsp;**Best season/month**

- Late April to early May for cherry blossoms.
- Autumn for foliage and fewer crowds.
