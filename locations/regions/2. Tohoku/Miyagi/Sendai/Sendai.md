**Sendai**

Sendai is Tohoku's main city hub with broad boulevards, shopping arcades, and strong festival culture.

&emsp;&emsp;**Suggested order (1-2 days)**

- Aoba Castle site and city view
- Downtown arcades and local food
- Zuihoden and nearby temple zones

Festival details: [Tanabata Festival](../../../../../events/festivals/Tanabata%20Festival.md)

&emsp;&emsp;**Logistics**

- Access from Tokyo: Tohoku Shinkansen.
- Typical travel time: 90-120 min.
- Typical one-way fare: JPY 10,500-12,000.

&emsp;&emsp;**Best season/month**

- August for Tanabata Festival.
- October-November for comfortable weather.
