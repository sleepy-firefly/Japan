**Matsushima**

Matsushima is one of Japan's classic scenic bay destinations with pine islands, temples, and boat cruises.

&emsp;&emsp;**Top places**

- Matsushima Bay cruise
- Zuigan-ji Temple
- Entsuin and waterfront walks

&emsp;&emsp;**Logistics**

- Access from Sendai: JR Senseki Line.
- Typical travel time: 40-50 min.
- Typical one-way fare: JPY 420-600.

&emsp;&emsp;**Best season**

- Spring and autumn for mild weather and scenery.
