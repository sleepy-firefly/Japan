**Morioka**

Morioka is a calm regional city with castle-park remains, local noodle culture, and good onward connections.

&emsp;&emsp;**Top places**

- Morioka Castle Ruins Park
- Nakanohashi and old district walks
- Local noodle spots (wanko soba, reimen, jajamen)

&emsp;&emsp;**Logistics**

- Access from Tokyo: Tohoku Shinkansen.
- Typical travel time: 2-2.5 hours.
- Typical one-way fare: JPY 14,000-15,500.

&emsp;&emsp;**Best season**

- May-June and October-November for city walks.
