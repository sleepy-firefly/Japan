**Hiraizumi**

Hiraizumi is a UNESCO-listed historical area with major Buddhist heritage sites.

&emsp;&emsp;**Top places**

- Chuson-ji Temple
- Motsu-ji Temple and gardens
- Geibikei gorge side trip (optional)

&emsp;&emsp;**Logistics**

- Access from Sendai or Morioka by JR and local transfer.
- Typical travel time from Sendai: 1.5-2 hours.
- Typical one-way fare: JPY 2,500-4,500.

&emsp;&emsp;**Best season**

- Spring and autumn for temple grounds and foliage.
