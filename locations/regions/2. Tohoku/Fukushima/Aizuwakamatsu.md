**Aizuwakamatsu**

Aizuwakamatsu is a strong samurai-heritage city with castles, traditional crafts, and mountain views.

&emsp;&emsp;**Top places**

- Tsuruga Castle
- Oyakuen garden
- Sake breweries and historic streets

&emsp;&emsp;**Logistics**

- Access from Tokyo/Sendai usually via Koriyama then local train.
- Typical one-way fare: JPY 4,500-10,000 depending on route.

&emsp;&emsp;**Best season**

- Spring for castle blossoms.
- Autumn for foliage and cooler weather.
