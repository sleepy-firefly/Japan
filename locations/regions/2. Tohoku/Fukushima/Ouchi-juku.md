**Ouchi-juku**

Ouchi-juku is a preserved post town with thatched-roof streets and Edo-era atmosphere.

&emsp;&emsp;**Top places**

- Main historic street
- Traditional soba houses
- Hilltop village viewpoint

&emsp;&emsp;**Logistics**

- Access: train to Yunokami Onsen area + local bus/taxi.
- Typical access cost varies widely by departure point.

&emsp;&emsp;**Best season**

- Winter for snow scenery.
- Late spring and autumn for comfortable walking.
