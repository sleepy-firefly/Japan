**Aomori City**

Aomori City is known for Nebuta culture, harbor walks, and easy gateways to northern Tohoku nature.

&emsp;&emsp;**Top places**

- Nebuta Museum Wa Rasse
- A-Factory and bay waterfront
- ASPAM observation area

Event guide: [Nebuta Festival](../../../../events/festivals/Nebuta%20Festival.md)


&emsp;&emsp;**Logistics**

- Access from Tokyo: Tohoku/Hokkaido Shinkansen to Shin-Aomori.
- Typical travel time: 3-3.5 hours.
- Typical one-way fare: JPY 17,000-18,500.

&emsp;&emsp;**Best season/month**

- August for Nebuta Festival.
- Spring and autumn for general sightseeing.
