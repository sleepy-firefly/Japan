**Hirosaki**

Hirosaki is famous for one of Japan's best castle-park cherry blossom scenes and a historic samurai-town atmosphere.

&emsp;&emsp;**Top places**

- Hirosaki Castle Park
- Fujita Memorial Garden
- Traditional district streets and cafes

&emsp;&emsp;**Logistics**

- Access from Aomori City: JR Ou Line to Hirosaki.
- Typical travel time: 35-50 min.
- Typical one-way fare: JPY 700-1,000.

&emsp;&emsp;**Best season/month**

- Late April to early May for cherry blossoms.
- October-November for autumn colors.
