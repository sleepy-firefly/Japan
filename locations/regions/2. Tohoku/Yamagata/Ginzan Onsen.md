**Ginzan Onsen**

Ginzan Onsen is a historic hot spring town with Taisho-era architecture and romantic evening streets.

&emsp;&emsp;**Top places**

- Riverfront ryokan street
- Public bathhouses
- Nearby old mine tunnel walk

&emsp;&emsp;**Logistics**

- Access: train to Oishida Station, then local bus.
- Typical final bus fare: around JPY 700-1,000.

&emsp;&emsp;**Best season**

- Winter for iconic snow-lit views.
- Autumn for cooler weather and fewer crowds.
