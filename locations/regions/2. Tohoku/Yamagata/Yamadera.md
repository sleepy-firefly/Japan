**Yamadera**

Yamadera is a mountain temple complex with dramatic stair climbs and valley views.

&emsp;&emsp;**Top places**

- Risshaku-ji Temple route
- Cliffside viewpoints
- Base-town soba stops

&emsp;&emsp;**Logistics**

- Access from Sendai or Yamagata by JR Senzan Line.
- Typical fare: JPY 250-1,200 depending on start point.

&emsp;&emsp;**Best season**

- May-June and October-November for comfortable hiking.
