**Kurayoshi**

Kurayoshi has preserved white-wall storehouses and a calm historical district atmosphere.

&emsp;&emsp;**Top places**

- Shirakabe Dozogun district
- Traditional craft and cafe stops
- Nearby hot spring side trip options

&emsp;&emsp;**Logistics**

- Access via local rail/bus links from Tottori or Yonago areas.

&emsp;&emsp;**Best season**

- Spring and autumn for old-town walks.
