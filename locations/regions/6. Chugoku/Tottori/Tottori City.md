**Tottori City**

Tottori City is known for coastal scenery and easy access to famous sand dunes.

&emsp;&emsp;**Top places**

- Tottori Sand Dunes
- Sand Museum
- Coastline viewpoints

&emsp;&emsp;**Logistics**

- Access from Osaka by JR limited express or highway bus.
- Typical one-way fare range: JPY 3,500-7,500.

&emsp;&emsp;**Best season**

- Spring and autumn for dune walking comfort.
