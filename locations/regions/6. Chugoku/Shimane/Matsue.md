**Matsue**

Matsue is known for its castle, tea culture, and lakeside atmosphere.

&emsp;&emsp;**Top places**

- Matsue Castle
- Horikawa moat boat area
- Lafcadio Hearn former residence district

&emsp;&emsp;**Logistics**

- Access from Okayama/Hiroshima by JR limited express with transfers.
- Fares and times vary by starting city.

&emsp;&emsp;**Best season**

- Spring and autumn for castle and lakeside routes.
