**Izumo**

Izumo is one of Japan's key spiritual destinations, centered on Izumo Taisha shrine.

&emsp;&emsp;**Top places**

- Izumo Taisha
- Inasa Beach
- Local soba and station area routes

&emsp;&emsp;**Logistics**

- Access from Matsue by Ichibata Railway or bus routes.
- Typical one-way fare range: JPY 500-1,200.

&emsp;&emsp;**Best season**

- Year-round pilgrimage destination.
- Best comfort in spring and autumn.
