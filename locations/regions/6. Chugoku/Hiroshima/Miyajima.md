**Miyajima**

Miyajima is one of Japan's classic sacred-island destinations with shrine gates and mountain trails.

&emsp;&emsp;**Top places**

- Itsukushima Shrine and floating torii
- Omotesando street
- Mt. Misen ropeway/hike

&emsp;&emsp;**Logistics**

- Access from Hiroshima: JR to Miyajimaguchi + ferry.
- Typical one-way total: around JPY 620-900.

&emsp;&emsp;**Best season**

- Spring and autumn for mild weather.
- Avoid peak holiday congestion when possible.
