**Onomichi**

Onomichi is a hillside port town; for temple-focused picks see the [Temple Index](../../../temples/Temple%20Index.md).

&emsp;&emsp;**Top places**

- Temple walk route (see [Temple Index](../../../temples/Temple%20Index.md))
- Cat alley and old streets
- Seaside promenade

&emsp;&emsp;**Logistics**

- Access from Hiroshima/Fukuyama by JR Sanyo Line.
- Typical one-way fare from Hiroshima area: around JPY 1,500-2,500.

&emsp;&emsp;**Best season**

- Spring and autumn for walking and cycling comfort.
