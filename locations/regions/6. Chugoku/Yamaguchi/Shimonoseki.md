**Shimonoseki**

Shimonoseki is a strait-side city known for seafood markets and views toward northern Kyushu.

&emsp;&emsp;**Top places**

- Karato Market
- Kanmon strait waterfront
- Marine and historical museum areas

&emsp;&emsp;**Logistics**

- Access from Hiroshima/Okayama by JR Shinkansen + local transfer.
- Fares vary by departure city.

&emsp;&emsp;**Best season**

- Spring and autumn for coastal walking.
