**Iwakuni**

Iwakuni is famous for Kintai Bridge and samurai-era riverside scenery.

&emsp;&emsp;**Top places**

- Kintai Bridge
- Iwakuni Castle ropeway area
- Old quarter around bridge approaches

&emsp;&emsp;**Logistics**

- Access from Hiroshima by JR Sanyo Line/Shinkansen + local.
- Typical one-way fare range: JPY 800-3,500.

&emsp;&emsp;**Best season**

- Spring blossoms and autumn foliage are both excellent.
