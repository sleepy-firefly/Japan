**Kurashiki**

Kurashiki is known for its beautiful canal district and historical merchant architecture.

&emsp;&emsp;**Top places**

- Bikan historical quarter
- Canal area evening walk
- Local craft and denim shops

&emsp;&emsp;**Logistics**

- Access from Okayama by JR local rapid.
- Typical travel time: 15-20 min.
- Typical one-way fare: JPY 330-500.

&emsp;&emsp;**Best season**

- Spring and autumn for comfortable old-town walks.
