**Naoshima Access Base (Okayama Side)**

Okayama and Uno are practical bases for reaching Naoshima art-island ferries.

&emsp;&emsp;**Top places**

- Uno Port area
- Ferry routes to Naoshima
- Return options via Takamatsu side

&emsp;&emsp;**Logistics**

- Access: JR to Uno Station, then walk to ferry terminal.
- Ferry fares and schedules vary by season/day.

&emsp;&emsp;**Best season**

- Spring and autumn for island art visits and cycling.
