**Okayama City**

Okayama is a convenient Chugoku base with one of Japan's top gardens and easy rail access to Kurashiki and Naoshima routes.

&emsp;&emsp;**Suggested order (full day)**

- Korakuen Garden
- Okayama Castle area
- Local arcade streets and cafes

&emsp;&emsp;**Logistics**

- Access from Osaka: Sanyo Shinkansen.
- Typical travel time: 45-60 min.
- Typical one-way fare: JPY 6,000-7,000.

&emsp;&emsp;**Where to stay**

- Around Okayama Station for day-trip convenience.

&emsp;&emsp;**Best season**

- Spring and autumn for garden scenery and easier walking weather.
