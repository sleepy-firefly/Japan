**Kawaguchiko**

Kawaguchiko is one of the best Mt. Fuji view bases with lake scenery and easy day-hike options.

&emsp;&emsp;**Top places**

- Lake Kawaguchi viewpoints
- Chureito Pagoda area (nearby)
- Ropeway and lakeside museums

&emsp;&emsp;**Logistics**

- Access from Tokyo by limited express or highway bus.
- Typical travel time: 2-2.5 hours.
- Typical one-way fare: JPY 2,000-4,500.

&emsp;&emsp;**Best season/month**

- November-February for clearer Mt. Fuji views.
- April for blossoms and mixed scenery.
