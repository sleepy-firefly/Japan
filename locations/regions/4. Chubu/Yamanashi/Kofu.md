**Kofu**

Kofu is a regional city known for wine, fruit farms, and mountain views.

&emsp;&emsp;**Top places**

- Takeda Shrine
- Kofu Castle park area
- Winery and seasonal fruit activities

&emsp;&emsp;**Logistics**

- Access from Shinjuku by JR Chuo Limited Express.
- Typical travel time: 90-110 min.
- Typical one-way fare: JPY 2,500-4,500.

&emsp;&emsp;**Best season**

- Spring for blossoms and vineyards.
- Autumn for harvest and clear weather.
