**Kamikochi**

Kamikochi is one of Japan's top alpine valley destinations for easy to moderate scenic hikes.

&emsp;&emsp;**Top places**

- Kappa Bridge area
- Taisho Pond and Myojin route
- Mountain panorama viewpoints

&emsp;&emsp;**Logistics**

- Access requires bus transfer from Matsumoto/Shin-Shimashima.
- Private cars are restricted in core area.
- Typical transport cost varies by start point and season.

&emsp;&emsp;**Best season/month**

- June-October (closed in winter).
- October for autumn colors.
