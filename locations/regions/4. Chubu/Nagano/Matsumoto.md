**Matsumoto**

Matsumoto is famous for one of Japan's best original castles and easy access to mountain areas.

&emsp;&emsp;**Top places**

- Matsumoto Castle
- Nakamachi old streets
- Art museum district

&emsp;&emsp;**Logistics**

- Access from Tokyo by JR Azusa limited express.
- Typical travel time: 2.5-3 hours.
- Typical one-way fare: JPY 6,500-7,500.

&emsp;&emsp;**Best season**

- Spring and autumn for castle views.
- Summer for nearby mountain routes.
