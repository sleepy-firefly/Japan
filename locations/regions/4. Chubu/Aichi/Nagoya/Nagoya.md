**Nagoya**

Nagoya is a major Chubu city with castle history, modern shopping districts, and strong food culture.

&emsp;&emsp;**Suggested order (1-2 days)**

- Nagoya Castle and nearby gardens
- Sakae district and Osu area
- Night local food route (miso katsu, hitsumabushi)

&emsp;&emsp;**Logistics**

- Access from Tokyo/Kyoto by Tokaido Shinkansen.
- Typical one-way fare from Tokyo: JPY 10,500-12,000.

&emsp;&emsp;**Best season**

- March-May and October-November for city walks.
