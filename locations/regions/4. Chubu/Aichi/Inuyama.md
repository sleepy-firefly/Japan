**Inuyama**

Inuyama is known for its original castle keep and compact historic townscape.

&emsp;&emsp;**Top places**

- Inuyama Castle
- Castle town street food area
- Meiji Mura (short transfer)

&emsp;&emsp;**Logistics**

- Access from Nagoya by Meitetsu line.
- Typical travel time: 30-40 min.
- Typical one-way fare: JPY 600-700.

&emsp;&emsp;**Best season**

- Spring and autumn for castle views and walking weather.
