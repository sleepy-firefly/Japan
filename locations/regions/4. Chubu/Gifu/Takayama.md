**Takayama**

Takayama is a preserved mountain town with Edo-style streets, markets, and regional food.

&emsp;&emsp;**Top places**

- Sanmachi old town
- Morning markets
- Takayama Jinya and local sake shops

&emsp;&emsp;**Logistics**

- Access from Nagoya/Toyama by JR limited express.
- Typical one-way fare: JPY 3,000-6,500 depending on route.

&emsp;&emsp;**Best season**

- Spring and autumn for festivals and weather.
- Winter for snow-town atmosphere.
