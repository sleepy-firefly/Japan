**Shirakawa-go**

Shirakawa-go is a UNESCO village known for traditional gassho-zukuri farmhouses.

&emsp;&emsp;**Top places**

- Ogimachi village center
- Hilltop observatory
- Folk museum houses

&emsp;&emsp;**Logistics**

- Access mainly by bus from Takayama or Kanazawa.
- Typical one-way fare: JPY 2,000-2,600.

&emsp;&emsp;**Best season**

- Winter for snow-covered village views.
- Autumn for foliage and lighter weather.
