**Toyama City**

Toyama is a compact city used as a base for Alpine Route and Kurobe-area travel.

&emsp;&emsp;**Top places**

- Toyama Castle park area
- Glass Art Museum
- Riverside and seafood markets

&emsp;&emsp;**Logistics**

- Access from Tokyo: Hokuriku Shinkansen.
- Typical travel time: 2-2.5 hours.
- Typical one-way fare: JPY 12,000-13,500.

&emsp;&emsp;**Best season**

- Spring to autumn for onward mountain routes.
