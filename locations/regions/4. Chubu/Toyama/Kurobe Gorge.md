**Kurobe Gorge**

Kurobe Gorge is known for scenic mountain rail routes and dramatic river-valley views.

&emsp;&emsp;**Top places**

- Kurobe Gorge Railway
- Gorge viewpoints and short walks
- Onsen stops along route

&emsp;&emsp;**Logistics**

- Access from Toyama via local rail to Unazuki Onsen.
- Typical fare varies by section and season.

&emsp;&emsp;**Best season/month**

- October-November for peak foliage.
- Summer for greenery and easier weather.
