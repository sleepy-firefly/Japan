**Echigo-Yuzawa**

Echigo-Yuzawa is a major snow and onsen gateway with very easy Shinkansen access.

&emsp;&emsp;**Top places**

- Ski resorts and snow activities
- Onsen facilities
- Ponshukan sake area at station

&emsp;&emsp;**Logistics**

- Access from Tokyo: Joetsu Shinkansen.
- Typical travel time: 70-90 min.
- Typical one-way fare: JPY 6,500-7,500.

&emsp;&emsp;**Best season**

- December-March for snow season.
- Green season for light mountain walks.
