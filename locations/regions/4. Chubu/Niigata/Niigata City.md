**Niigata City**

Niigata City is a coastal city known for seafood, sake culture, and riverfront walks.

&emsp;&emsp;**Top places**

- Bandai Bridge and waterfront
- Sake tasting districts
- Local market areas

&emsp;&emsp;**Logistics**

- Access from Tokyo: Joetsu Shinkansen.
- Typical travel time: 2-2.5 hours.
- Typical one-way fare: JPY 10,000-11,000.

&emsp;&emsp;**Best season**

- Spring to autumn for city and coastal walking.
