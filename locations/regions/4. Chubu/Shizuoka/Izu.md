**Izu Peninsula**

Izu offers coastlines, onsen towns, and scenic train/bus routes with ocean views.

&emsp;&emsp;**Top places**

- Atami and Ito onsen zones
- Jogasaki Coast walks
- Shimoda beaches and historic quarter

&emsp;&emsp;**Logistics**

- Access from Tokyo by JR/Izukyu routes or highway options.
- Travel time and fare vary strongly by exact destination.

&emsp;&emsp;**Best season**

- Spring and autumn for mixed outdoor sightseeing.
- Summer for beaches.
