**Shizuoka City**

Shizuoka City is a transit-friendly stop with tea culture, coastal views, and Mt. Fuji-side day trips.

&emsp;&emsp;**Top places**

- Sumpu Castle Park area
- Nihondaira viewpoints
- Local seafood and tea stops

&emsp;&emsp;**Logistics**

- Access from Tokyo: Tokaido Shinkansen.
- Typical travel time: 60-75 min.
- Typical one-way fare: JPY 6,000-7,000.

&emsp;&emsp;**Best season**

- Spring and autumn for clear views and city walks.
