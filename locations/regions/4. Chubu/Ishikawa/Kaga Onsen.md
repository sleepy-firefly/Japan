**Kaga Onsen**

Kaga Onsen is a cluster of hot spring towns with traditional inns and craft culture.

&emsp;&emsp;**Top places**

- Yamashiro Onsen and Yamanaka Onsen streets
- Public baths and ryokan stays
- Traditional lacquer/craft areas

&emsp;&emsp;**Logistics**

- Access from Kanazawa by JR limited express/local train.
- Typical fare: JPY 1,000-2,500 one way.

&emsp;&emsp;**Best season**

- Year-round for onsen.
- Autumn for cooler walks and foliage.
