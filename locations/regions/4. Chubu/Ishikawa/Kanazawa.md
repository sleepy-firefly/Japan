**Kanazawa**

Kanazawa is a top culture destination with gardens, samurai/tea districts, and strong regional cuisine.

&emsp;&emsp;**Top places**

- Kenroku-en Garden
- Kanazawa Castle Park
- Higashi Chaya and Nagamachi districts

&emsp;&emsp;**Logistics**

- Access from Tokyo: Hokuriku Shinkansen.
- Typical travel time: 2.5-3 hours.
- Typical one-way fare: JPY 14,000-15,500.

&emsp;&emsp;**Best season**

- Spring and autumn for gardens and historic walks.
