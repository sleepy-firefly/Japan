**Enoshima and Fujisawa**

This coastal area is great for sea views, island walks, shrines, and relaxed beach-town atmosphere.

&emsp;&emsp;**Suggested order (day trip)**

- Katase-Enoshima Station area
- Enoshima Shrine and island paths
- Sea Candle observatory
- Beach and cafes in Shonan area

&emsp;&emsp;**Logistics**

- Access from Shinjuku: Odakyu line to Fujisawa/Katase-Enoshima.
- Typical travel time: 65-85 min.
- Typical fare: JPY 650-1,250 depending on train type.
- Enoden line connects to Kamakura for combined itinerary.

&emsp;&emsp;**Where to stay**

- Fujisawa Station area for practical rail access.
- Enoshima side for beach-oriented overnight stay.

&emsp;&emsp;**Best season**

- May-June and September-October for milder weather.
