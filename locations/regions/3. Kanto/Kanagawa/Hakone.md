**Hakone**

Hakone is a classic onsen and nature destination with lake views, mountain ropeways, and museums.

&emsp;&emsp;**Suggested order (1-2 days)**

- Day 1: Hakone-Yumoto -> Open-Air Museum -> Gora onsen
- Day 2: Ropeway -> Owakudani -> Lake Ashi cruise -> Hakone Shrine

&emsp;&emsp;**Logistics**

- Access from Shinjuku: Odakyu Romancecar to Hakone-Yumoto.
- Typical travel time: 85-95 min.
- Typical fare: JPY 2,470-2,700 one way.
- Hakone Freepass can reduce costs for loop transport.

&emsp;&emsp;**Where to stay**

- Hakone-Yumoto for easiest transport.
- Gora for stronger onsen focus.

&emsp;&emsp;**Best season/month**

- October-November for foliage.
- March-April for clearer views and spring weather.
