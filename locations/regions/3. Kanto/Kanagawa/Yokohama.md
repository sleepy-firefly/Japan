**Yokohama**

Yokohama is a major port city with waterfront parks, Chinatown, museums, and modern skyline views.

&emsp;&emsp;**Suggested order (1-2 days)**

- Morning: Minato Mirai waterfront and Red Brick Warehouse
- Afternoon: Cup Noodles Museum or Yamashita Park
- Evening: Chinatown dinner and bay night views

&emsp;&emsp;**Logistics**

- Access from Tokyo: JR Tokaido Line, Yokosuka Line, Keihin-Tohoku Line, Tokyu Toyoko Line.
- Typical travel time: 25-45 min.
- Typical fare: JPY 480-700 one way.

&emsp;&emsp;**Where to stay**

- Minato Mirai: scenic but pricier.
- Yokohama Station area: best transport convenience.

&emsp;&emsp;**Best season**

- April-May and October-November for pleasant bay walks.
