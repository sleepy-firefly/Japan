**Kamakura**

Kamakura is one of the best day trips near Tokyo; for temple details see [Hase-dera (Kamakura)](../../../temples/Hase-dera%20(Kamakura).md) and [Meigetsu-in](../../../../temples/Meigetsu-in.md).

&emsp;&emsp;**Suggested order (full day)**

- Kotoku-in (Great Buddha)
- Hase-dera Temple
- Tsurugaoka Hachimangu Shrine
- Komachi Street for food and snacks
- Optional sunset at Yuigahama beach

&emsp;&emsp;**Logistics**

- Access from Tokyo: JR Yokosuka Line to Kamakura Station.
- Typical travel time: 55-70 min.
- Typical fare: JPY 940-1,000 one way.
- Enoden line is useful for Hase and Enoshima side spots.

&emsp;&emsp;**Where to stay**

- Around Kamakura Station for temple sightseeing.
- Enoshima/Fujisawa for beach atmosphere.

&emsp;&emsp;**Best season/month**

- June for hydrangeas.
- November for autumn leaves.
- Spring is best for mixed weather and walking.
