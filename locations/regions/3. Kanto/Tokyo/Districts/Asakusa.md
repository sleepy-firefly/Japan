**Asakusa (Tokyo District)**

Asakusa is one of Tokyo's best traditional areas; for temple details see [Senso-ji](../../../../../temples/Senso-ji.md).

&emsp;&emsp;**What to see (in order)**

- Kaminarimon Gate
- Nakamise shopping street
- Senso-ji Temple main hall and grounds
- Sumida River walk and river cruise optional

&emsp;&emsp;**Practical info**

- Access: Tokyo Metro Ginza Line (G19), Toei Asakusa Line (A18), Tobu Skytree Line.
- Typical fare from central Tokyo: JPY 180-260.
- Suggested visit window: 08:00-11:00 for fewer crowds.

&emsp;&emsp;**Where to stay nearby**

- Asakusa: best for budget-midrange hotels.
- Ueno: strong rail connections for day trips.

&emsp;&emsp;**Best season/month**

- March-April for cherry blossoms near Sumida.
- May and November for milder weather and festival walks.
