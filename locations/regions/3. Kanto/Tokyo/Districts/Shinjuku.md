**Shinjuku (Tokyo District)**

Shinjuku is a major transport hub with parks, department stores, nightlife lanes, and easy day-trip departures.

&emsp;&emsp;**What to see (in order)**

- Shinjuku Gyoen National Garden
- Department store district around station exits
- Omoide Yokocho and Golden Gai in the evening
- Tokyo Metropolitan Government Building observation deck

&emsp;&emsp;**Practical info**

- Access: JR Yamanote/Chuo/Saikyo lines, multiple metro and private rail lines.
- Typical fare from Tokyo Station/Ueno: JPY 180-260.
- Last trains vary by line but often near midnight.

&emsp;&emsp;**Where to stay nearby**

- Shinjuku Station west/south areas for transport convenience.
- Nakano for slightly lower hotel costs.

&emsp;&emsp;**Best season/month**

- March-April and November for park scenery and comfortable temperatures.
