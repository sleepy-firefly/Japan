**Shibuya (Tokyo District)**

Shibuya combines youth culture, shopping, food, and nightlife, with easy access to western Tokyo areas.

&emsp;&emsp;**What to see (in order)**

- Shibuya Scramble Crossing and Hachiko area
- Shibuya Sky or other city-view deck
- Cat Street and side streets toward Harajuku
- Evening food streets and bars

&emsp;&emsp;**Practical info**

- Access: JR Yamanote Line, Tokyo Metro Ginza/Hanzomon/Fukutoshin lines.
- Typical fare from Tokyo/Ueno: JPY 180-260.
- Peak crowd periods: 17:00-21:00 and weekends.

&emsp;&emsp;**Where to stay nearby**

- Shibuya: convenient but pricier.
- Ebisu or Shin-Okubo: often better value with good access.

&emsp;&emsp;**Best season/month**

- Year-round.
- October-November is best for comfortable city walking.
