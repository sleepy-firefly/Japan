**Tokyo (City Overview)**

Tokyo is a huge city with historic temples, modern districts, major museums, parks, and strong otaku culture. For museum details see the [Museum Index](../../../../museums/Museum%20Index.md); for major convention timing see [AnimeJapan](../../../../events/otaku/AnimeJapan.md), [Tokyo Game Show](../../../../events/otaku/Tokyo%20Game%20Show.md), and [Comiket](../../../../events/otaku/Comiket.md).

&emsp;&emsp;**Suggested order (4 days)**

- Day 1: Asakusa -> Ueno -> Akihabara
- Day 2: Meiji Jingu -> Harajuku -> Shibuya
- Day 3: Shinjuku -> Omoide Yokocho -> Tokyo Metropolitan Government Building night view
- Day 4: Odaiba or teamLab area -> Ginza/Tokyo Station

&emsp;&emsp;**Key transport/logistics**

- Main lines: JR Yamanote Line (loop), Tokyo Metro Ginza Line (G), Hibiya Line (H), Toei Asakusa Line (A).
- Typical local fare: JPY 180-320 per ride.
- First trains are usually around 05:00; last trains around 00:00-00:30 (varies by line).

&emsp;&emsp;**Where to stay**

- Ueno/Asakusa: best value and easy airport access.
- Shinjuku/Shibuya: best nightlife and many direct train options.
- Tokyo Station area: best for Shinkansen day trips.

&emsp;&emsp;**Best season**

- March-April: cherry blossoms.
- October-November: mild weather and autumn colors.
- December-February: clearer skies and fewer crowds (except New Year).
