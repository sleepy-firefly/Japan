**Utsunomiya**

Utsunomiya is a practical stop known for gyoza food culture and as a gateway to northern Kanto.

&emsp;&emsp;**Suggested order (half-full day)**

- Gyoza-focused lunch route near station
- Oya History Museum and Oya Temple area
- Short city center evening walk

&emsp;&emsp;**Logistics**

- Access from Tokyo/Ueno: JR Utsunomiya Line or Shinkansen.
- Typical travel time: 50-100 min depending on train type.
- Typical fare: JPY 1,980-4,800 one way.

&emsp;&emsp;**Where to stay**

- Around Utsunomiya Station for easy onward transfers.

&emsp;&emsp;**Best season**

- Year-round city destination; spring and autumn are most comfortable.
