**Nasu**

Nasu is a highland resort area with nature walks, hot springs, and family-friendly attractions.

&emsp;&emsp;**Suggested order (1-2 days)**

- Nasu Ropeway and mountain area
- Onsen stay and local dairy/food stops
- Nasu Heisei-no-Mori forest walks

&emsp;&emsp;**Logistics**

- Access from Tokyo: Tohoku Shinkansen to Nasushiobara, then local bus/car.
- Typical total travel time: 90-130 min.
- Typical fare: JPY 6,000-7,000 one way to Nasushiobara (plus local transport).

&emsp;&emsp;**Where to stay**

- Nasu onsen area for mountain and hot spring access.

&emsp;&emsp;**Best season/month**

- May-June and October for best weather and scenery.
