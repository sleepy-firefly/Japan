**Chichibu**

Chichibu offers mountain scenery, shrines, seasonal flowers, and a more rural side of the Kanto region.

&emsp;&emsp;**Suggested order (1-2 days)**

- Chichibu Shrine
- Hitsujiyama Park (shibazakura season)
- Nagatoro line/river area (optional side trip)

&emsp;&emsp;**Logistics**

- Access from Ikebukuro: Seibu Limited Express to Seibu-Chichibu.
- Typical travel time: 80-95 min.
- Typical fare: JPY 1,500-2,200 one way depending on seat type.

&emsp;&emsp;**Where to stay**

- Seibu-Chichibu area for easy rail access.
- Ryokan/onsen lodgings for nature-focused trip.

&emsp;&emsp;**Best season/month**

- April-May for flower season.
- November for mountain autumn colors.
