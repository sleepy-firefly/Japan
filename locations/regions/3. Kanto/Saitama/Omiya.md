**Omiya (Saitama City)**

Omiya is a practical city stop with rail museums and strong transport links; see [Railway Museum (Omiya)](../../../museums/Railway%20Museum%20(Omiya).md) for details.

&emsp;&emsp;**Suggested order (half-full day)**

- Railway Museum (see [Railway Museum (Omiya)](../../../museums/Railway%20Museum%20(Omiya).md))
- Omiya Bonsai Village and museum
- Hikawa Shrine

&emsp;&emsp;**Logistics**

- Access from Tokyo/Ueno: JR Utsunomiya or Takasaki lines.
- Typical travel time: 25-40 min.
- Typical fare: JPY 480-700 one way.

&emsp;&emsp;**Where to stay**

- Near Omiya Station if using it as transfer base.

&emsp;&emsp;**Best season**

- Year-round; spring and autumn are best for shrine/park walks.
