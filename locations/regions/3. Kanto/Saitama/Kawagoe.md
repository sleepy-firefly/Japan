**Kawagoe**

Kawagoe is known as "Little Edo" for its preserved warehouse streets and classic town atmosphere.

&emsp;&emsp;**Suggested order (day trip)**

- Kurazukuri old warehouse district
- Toki no Kane bell tower
- Kashiya Yokocho candy alley
- Hikawa Shrine

&emsp;&emsp;**Logistics**

- Access from Ikebukuro/Shinjuku: Tobu Tojo Line or Seibu line + transfer.
- Typical travel time: 30-60 min.
- Typical fare: JPY 500-1,000 one way.

&emsp;&emsp;**Where to stay**

- Usually a day trip from Tokyo.
- Overnight near Kawagoe Station for early morning photography.

&emsp;&emsp;**Best season**

- October-November for festivals and cooler weather.
- Spring for easier walking temperatures.
