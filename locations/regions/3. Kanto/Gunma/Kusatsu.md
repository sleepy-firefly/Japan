**Kusatsu Onsen**

Kusatsu is one of Japan's most famous hot spring towns, known for strong sulfur waters and classic onsen streets.

&emsp;&emsp;**Suggested order (1-2 days)**

- Yubatake hot spring field
- Sainokawara Park and open-air bath area
- Evening yukata walk in town center

&emsp;&emsp;**Logistics**

- Access from Tokyo: limited express/train to Naganohara-Kusatsuguchi + bus, or direct highway bus.
- Typical travel time: 3-4 hours.
- Typical fare: JPY 3,500-7,500 one way depending on mode.

&emsp;&emsp;**Where to stay**

- Around Yubatake for best walkability and atmosphere.

&emsp;&emsp;**Best season/month**

- Winter for snowy onsen atmosphere.
- Autumn for cool weather and foliage.
