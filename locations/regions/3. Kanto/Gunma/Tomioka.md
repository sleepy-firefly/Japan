**Tomioka**

Tomioka is known for the UNESCO-listed Tomioka Silk Mill and Meiji-era industrial heritage.

&emsp;&emsp;**Suggested order (half-full day)**

- Tomioka Silk Mill
- Local historic streets and cafes
- Optional side trip to nearby shrines/onsen towns

&emsp;&emsp;**Logistics**

- Access from Tokyo: Shinkansen/local train to Takasaki, then Joshin Dentetsu line to Joshu-Tomioka.
- Typical travel time: 2-2.5 hours.
- Typical fare: JPY 2,500-5,000 one way depending on route.

&emsp;&emsp;**Where to stay**

- Day trip from Takasaki or Tokyo.
- Stay in Takasaki for easier onward rail travel.

&emsp;&emsp;**Best season**

- Year-round, with spring and autumn best for town walks.
