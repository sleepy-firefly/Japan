**Minakami**

Minakami is an outdoor destination for rafting, hiking, ropeway views, and mountain hot springs.

&emsp;&emsp;**Suggested order (1-2 days)**

- Ropeway or mountain viewpoint area
- River activities (seasonal)
- Onsen evening in town

&emsp;&emsp;**Logistics**

- Access from Tokyo: Joetsu Shinkansen to Jomo-Kogen + bus/taxi, or local JR route to Minakami.
- Typical travel time: 70-140 min depending on route.
- Typical fare: JPY 2,500-6,000 one way.

&emsp;&emsp;**Where to stay**

- Minakami Onsen area.
- Takaragawa area for scenic ryokan stays.

&emsp;&emsp;**Best season/month**

- June-September for outdoor activities.
- January-March for winter scenery and snow sports.
