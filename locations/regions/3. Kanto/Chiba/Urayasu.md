**Urayasu (Tokyo Disney Resort Area)**

Urayasu is known for Tokyo DisneySea/Disneyland and resort hotels, with easy access from central Tokyo.

&emsp;&emsp;**Suggested order (1-2 days)**

- Day 1: Tokyo DisneySea
- Day 2: Tokyo Disneyland or Ikspiari + bay area

&emsp;&emsp;**Logistics**

- Access from Tokyo Station: JR Keiyo Line to Maihama.
- Typical travel time: 15-25 min.
- Typical fare: around JPY 230 one way.
- Park ticket prices vary by date; usually around JPY 7,900-10,900 per day.

&emsp;&emsp;**Where to stay**

- Maihama official resort hotels for convenience.
- Shin-Urayasu for cheaper alternatives.

&emsp;&emsp;**Best season/month**

- Mid-week in January-February or June for lighter crowds.
- October-December for seasonal park events.
