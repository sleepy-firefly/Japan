**Sawara (Katori)**

Sawara is a preserved canal town with Edo-period atmosphere, old merchant houses, and a slower pace.

&emsp;&emsp;**Suggested order (half-full day)**

- Historic canal district walk
- Small boat ride (seasonal operation)
- Katori Jingu Shrine (short transfer)

Festival details: [Sawara Grand Festival](../../../../events/festivals/Sawara%20Grand%20Festival.md)


&emsp;&emsp;**Logistics**

- Access from Tokyo: JR Sobu/Narita lines with transfer toward Sawara.
- Typical travel time: 100-140 min.
- Typical fare: around JPY 1,700-2,200 one way.

&emsp;&emsp;**Where to stay**

- Day trip from Tokyo or Narita.
- Local inn/guesthouse for quiet evening atmosphere.

&emsp;&emsp;**Best season/month**

- July for Sawara Grand Festival periods.
- Spring and autumn for comfortable walking.
