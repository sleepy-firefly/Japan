**Narita**

Narita is more than an airport city: it has a historic temple area, old shopping street, and local eel cuisine.

&emsp;&emsp;**Suggested order (half day)**

- Omotesando old street
- Naritasan Shinsho-ji Temple
- Naritasan Park

&emsp;&emsp;**Logistics**

- Access from Tokyo: JR Sobu/Narita lines or Keisei services.
- Typical travel time: 60-90 min.
- Typical fare: JPY 1,000-3,100 depending on service.

&emsp;&emsp;**Where to stay**

- Narita Station area for town visits.
- Airport hotels for flight convenience.

&emsp;&emsp;**Best season**

- March-April and October-November for temple walks.
