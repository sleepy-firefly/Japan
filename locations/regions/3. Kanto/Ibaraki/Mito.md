**Mito**

Mito is famous for Kairakuen Garden (one of Japan's top three gardens) and local history.

&emsp;&emsp;**Suggested order (day trip)**

- Kairakuen Garden
- Kobuntei historic villa
- Mito Castle ruins area

&emsp;&emsp;**Logistics**

- Access from Tokyo/Ueno: JR Joban Line Limited Express.
- Typical travel time: 70-90 min.
- Typical fare: JPY 3,000-4,000 one way.

&emsp;&emsp;**Where to stay**

- Around Mito Station for easy local transport.

&emsp;&emsp;**Best season/month**

- February-March for plum blossoms at Kairakuen.
- Autumn also pleasant for garden visits.
