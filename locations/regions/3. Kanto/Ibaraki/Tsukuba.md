**Tsukuba**

Tsukuba combines science-city attractions with mountain hiking at Mt. Tsukuba.

&emsp;&emsp;**Suggested order (day trip)**

- Tsukuba Space Center (JAXA-related visits)
- Mt. Tsukuba ropeway/cable car and summit area
- Onsen or local restaurants after hiking

&emsp;&emsp;**Logistics**

- Access from Akihabara: Tsukuba Express to Tsukuba Station.
- Typical travel time: 45-55 min.
- Typical fare: around JPY 1,210 one way.
- Buses to mountain area depart from Tsukuba Station.

&emsp;&emsp;**Where to stay**

- Near Tsukuba Station for transport convenience.
- Mt. Tsukuba area for nature-focused overnight stay.

&emsp;&emsp;**Best season**

- March-May and October-November for mountain hiking conditions.
