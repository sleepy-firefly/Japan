**Hitachinaka**

Hitachinaka is best known for Hitachi Seaside Park and its seasonal flower landscapes.

&emsp;&emsp;**Suggested order (day trip)**

- Hitachi Seaside Park flower zones
- Miharashi Hill viewpoints
- Ajigaura coast (optional)

&emsp;&emsp;**Logistics**

- Access: JR Joban Line to Katsuta, then bus to park.
- Typical travel time from Tokyo: 2-2.5 hours total.
- Typical fare: JPY 2,500-4,500 one way depending on train type, plus local bus.

&emsp;&emsp;**Where to stay**

- Day trip from Mito or Tokyo.
- Katsuta area for early park access.

&emsp;&emsp;**Best season/month**

- April-May for nemophila.
- October for kochia red hills.
