**Dogo Onsen**

Dogo Onsen is one of Japan's oldest hot spring areas with classic bathhouse architecture.

&emsp;&emsp;**Top places**

- Dogo Onsen Honkan area
- Shopping arcade and snack streets
- Evening yukata walks

&emsp;&emsp;**Logistics**

- Access from Matsuyama by city tram.
- Typical fare: around JPY 200-300.

&emsp;&emsp;**Best season**

- Year-round onsen destination.
- Autumn and winter feel especially atmospheric.
