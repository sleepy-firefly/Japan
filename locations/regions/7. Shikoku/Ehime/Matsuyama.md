**Matsuyama**

Matsuyama offers castle views, tram-based city travel, and traditional bathing culture.

&emsp;&emsp;**Top places**

- Matsuyama Castle
- Ropeway shopping street
- Dogo area evening walk

&emsp;&emsp;**Logistics**

- Access from Okayama/Hiroshima by train/ferry combinations or flights.
- City transport is easy via tram lines.

&emsp;&emsp;**Best season**

- Spring and autumn for city and castle routes.
