**Kochi City**

Kochi City is known for castle views, lively food markets, and Pacific-facing culture.

&emsp;&emsp;**Top places**

- Kochi Castle
- Hirome Market
- River and old-town walks

&emsp;&emsp;**Logistics**

- Access from Takamatsu/Tokushima by JR + limited express or highway bus.
- Travel times are longer than mainland routes; plan buffers.

&emsp;&emsp;**Best season**

- Spring and autumn for city walking.
- Summer for festival atmosphere.
