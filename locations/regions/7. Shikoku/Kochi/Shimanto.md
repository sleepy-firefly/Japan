**Shimanto**

Shimanto is known for clear rivers, cycling routes, and rural scenery in southwestern Kochi.

&emsp;&emsp;**Top places**

- Shimanto River viewpoints
- Chinkabashi low-water bridges
- Riverside cycling and canoe options

&emsp;&emsp;**Logistics**

- Access from Kochi City by train/bus with longer travel times.
- Plan full-day movement for in/out transit.

&emsp;&emsp;**Best season**

- May-October for river activities.
- Autumn for cooler cycling weather.
