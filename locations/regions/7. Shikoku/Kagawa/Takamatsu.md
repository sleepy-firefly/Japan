**Takamatsu**

Takamatsu is a practical Shikoku base with gardens, port access, and island connections.

&emsp;&emsp;**Top places**

- Ritsurin Garden
- Port area and local markets
- Day-trip ferry links to art islands

&emsp;&emsp;**Logistics**

- Access from Okayama by JR via Marine Liner.
- Typical travel time: 55-70 min.
- Typical one-way fare: JPY 1,500-2,000.

&emsp;&emsp;**Best season**

- Spring and autumn for gardens and ferry day trips.
