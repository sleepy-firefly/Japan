**Naoshima (Kagawa Side Access)**

Naoshima is one of Japan's best-known contemporary art islands.

&emsp;&emsp;**Top places**

- Benesse House area
- Chichu Art Museum (see [Chichu Art Museum (Naoshima)](../../../museums/Chichu%20Art%20Museum%20(Naoshima).md); reservation often needed)
- Port-area art installations

&emsp;&emsp;**Logistics**

- Access from Takamatsu or Uno by ferry.
- Ferry schedule varies by day and season.

&emsp;&emsp;**Best season**

- Spring and autumn for cycling and island walks.
