**Kotohira**

Kotohira is known for Kompira-san shrine and long stone stair pilgrim routes.

&emsp;&emsp;**Top places**

- Kotohira-gu shrine climb
- Historic town streets
- Local udon and sweets shops

&emsp;&emsp;**Logistics**

- Access from Takamatsu by JR or Kotoden routes.
- Typical one-way fare: JPY 500-1,200.

&emsp;&emsp;**Best season**

- Spring and autumn for stair climbing comfort.
