**Iya Valley**

Iya Valley is a dramatic mountain area with deep gorges, vine bridges, and remote villages.

&emsp;&emsp;**Top places**

- Kazurabashi vine bridge
- Oboke/Koboke gorge viewpoints
- Mountain onsen stays

&emsp;&emsp;**Logistics**

- Access usually via train/bus from Tokushima or Takamatsu with transfers.
- Routes are slower; plan conservative transfer times.

&emsp;&emsp;**Best season**

- Autumn for foliage.
- Late spring for greener valley scenery.
