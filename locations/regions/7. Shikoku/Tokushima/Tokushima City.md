**Tokushima City**

Tokushima is the gateway to eastern Shikoku, known for Awa Odori dance culture and river-side city views.

&emsp;&emsp;**Suggested order (1 day)**

- Awa Odori Kaikan museum/performance
- Bizan Ropeway viewpoint
- Shinmachi riverside walk

Event guide: [Awa Odori Festival](../../../../events/festivals/Awa%20Odori%20Festival.md)


&emsp;&emsp;**Logistics**

- Access from Osaka/Kobe: highway bus is usually the simplest route.
- Typical travel time: 2.5-3 hours.
- Typical one-way fare: JPY 3,000-4,500.

&emsp;&emsp;**Where to stay**

- Around JR Tokushima Station for easy transfers.

&emsp;&emsp;**Best season/month**

- August for Awa Odori Festival.
- Spring and autumn for comfortable city walks.
