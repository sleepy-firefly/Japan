**Naruto**

Naruto is known for tidal whirlpools in the strait and bridge viewpoints.

&emsp;&emsp;**Top places**

- Naruto whirlpool boat routes
- Onaruto Bridge promenade (if open)
- Coastal park areas

&emsp;&emsp;**Logistics**

- Access from Tokushima City by JR/bus.
- Typical one-way fare: JPY 300-800.

&emsp;&emsp;**Best season/month**

- Spring and autumn for sightseeing comfort.
- Check daily tide timings for strongest whirlpools.
