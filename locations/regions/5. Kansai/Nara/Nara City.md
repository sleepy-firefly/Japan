**Nara City**

Nara City is a major cultural destination; for temple details see [Todai-ji](../../../../temples/Todai-ji.md).

&emsp;&emsp;**Top places**

- Todai-ji Temple
- Nara Park and Kasuga Taisha
- Naramachi old district

&emsp;&emsp;**Logistics**

- Access from Kyoto/Osaka by JR or Kintetsu.
- Typical one-way fare: JPY 700-1,200.

&emsp;&emsp;**Best season**

- Spring and autumn for park and temple routes.
