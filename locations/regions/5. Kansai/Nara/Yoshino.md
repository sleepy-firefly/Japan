**Yoshino**

Yoshino is one of Japan's iconic mountain blossom destinations and also strong in autumn.

&emsp;&emsp;**Top places**

- Yoshinoyama slope routes
- Temple/shrine viewpoints (see [Temple Index](../../../temples/Temple%20Index.md))
- Ropeway and mountain village lanes

&emsp;&emsp;**Logistics**

- Access from Osaka/Nara by Kintetsu line.
- Typical one-way fare range: JPY 1,000-2,000.

&emsp;&emsp;**Best season/month**

- Early to mid April for cherry blossoms.
- November for mountain foliage.
