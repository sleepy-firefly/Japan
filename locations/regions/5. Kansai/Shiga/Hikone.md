**Hikone**

Hikone is known for its original castle keep and scenic Lake Biwa-side setting.

&emsp;&emsp;**Top places**

- Hikone Castle
- Genkyuen Garden
- Castle-town street areas

&emsp;&emsp;**Logistics**

- Access from Kyoto/Osaka by JR lines.
- Typical one-way fare from Kyoto: around JPY 1,200-1,700.

&emsp;&emsp;**Best season**

- Spring blossoms and autumn foliage are both strong.
