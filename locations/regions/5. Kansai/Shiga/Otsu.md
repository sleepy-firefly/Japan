**Otsu**

Otsu is a convenient Lake Biwa city with quick links from Kyoto; for temple details see the [Temple Index](../../../temples/Temple%20Index.md).

&emsp;&emsp;**Top places**

- Lake Biwa waterfront
- Miidera and nearby temple areas (see [Temple Index](../../../temples/Temple%20Index.md))
- Sakamoto district side trip

&emsp;&emsp;**Logistics**

- Access from Kyoto by JR Biwako Line.
- Typical travel time: 10-20 min.
- Typical fare: JPY 200-400.

&emsp;&emsp;**Best season**

- Spring and autumn for lakeside and temple routes.
