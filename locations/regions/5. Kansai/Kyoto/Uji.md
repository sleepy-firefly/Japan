**Uji**

Uji is a historic tea town between Kyoto and Nara; for temple details see [Byodo-in](../../../../temples/Byodo-in.md).

&emsp;&emsp;**Top places**

- Byodo-in Temple
- Uji river area and tea shops
- Ujigami Shrine

&emsp;&emsp;**Logistics**

- Access from Kyoto by JR Nara Line or Keihan routes.
- Typical travel time: 20-35 min.
- Typical fare: JPY 240-450.

&emsp;&emsp;**Best season**

- Spring and autumn for sightseeing.
- Summer for tea/cafe-focused slower days.
