**Arashiyama (Kyoto District)**

Arashiyama combines temple gardens, bamboo paths, and river scenery.

&emsp;&emsp;**Highlights**

- Tenryu-ji Temple
- Bamboo grove area
- Togetsukyo Bridge and riverside

&emsp;&emsp;**Best season**

- Late March-April and November for peak scenery.
