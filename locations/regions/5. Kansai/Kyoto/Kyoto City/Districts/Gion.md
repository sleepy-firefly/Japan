**Gion (Kyoto District)**

Gion is Kyoto's iconic traditional district with historic streets, tea houses, and shrine access.

&emsp;&emsp;**Highlights**

- Hanamikoji and side alleys
- Yasaka Shrine and Maruyama Park
- Evening lantern-lit walks

&emsp;&emsp;**Best season**

- Spring and autumn evenings are most atmospheric.
