**Kyoto City**

Kyoto City is one of Japan's top cultural destinations; for major temple details see [Kiyomizu-dera](../../../../../temples/Kiyomizu-dera.md) and [Sanjusangendo](../../../../../temples/Sanjusangendo.md).

&emsp;&emsp;**Suggested order (2-3 days)**

- East Kyoto: Kiyomizu-dera and Higashiyama lanes
- Central/North: Nijo area and selected temples
- West: Arashiyama and riverside routes

&emsp;&emsp;**Logistics**

- Main transport: Kyoto City Bus, subway, JR and private lines.
- Typical local ride fare: around JPY 230 (bus) and JPY 220+ (subway).

&emsp;&emsp;**Best season/month**

- March-April for blossoms.
- November for autumn foliage.
