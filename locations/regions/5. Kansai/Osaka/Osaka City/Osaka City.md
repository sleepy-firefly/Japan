**Osaka City**

Osaka City is a major urban destination with food culture, nightlife, shopping, and easy day trips.

&emsp;&emsp;**Suggested order (2 days)**

- Day 1: Osaka Castle + Umeda area
- Day 2: Namba and Dotonbori + Shinsekai optional

&emsp;&emsp;**Logistics**

- Main transport: Osaka Metro + JR loop network.
- Typical metro fare: JPY 190-330.

&emsp;&emsp;**Best season**

- Year-round.
- Best comfort months are October-November and March-May.
