**Namba (Osaka District)**

Namba is one of Osaka's busiest entertainment and food districts.

&emsp;&emsp;**Highlights**

- Dotonbori area
- Kuromon market side streets
- Nightlife and izakaya zones

&emsp;&emsp;**Best season**

- Year-round evening destination.
