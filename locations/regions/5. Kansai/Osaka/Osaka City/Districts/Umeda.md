**Umeda (Osaka District)**

Umeda is Osaka's main business and transport hub with large malls and skyline views.

&emsp;&emsp;**Highlights**

- Osaka/Umeda station complexes
- Department stores and basement food halls
- Evening observatory viewpoints

&emsp;&emsp;**Best season**

- Year-round, especially useful in rainy weather due to indoor networks.
