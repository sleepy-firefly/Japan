**Sakai**

Sakai is known for kofun burial mounds, tea history, and a quieter side of Osaka Prefecture.

&emsp;&emsp;**Top places**

- Mozu Kofun cluster viewpoints
- Traditional knife craft areas
- Local tea culture spots

&emsp;&emsp;**Logistics**

- Access from central Osaka by Nankai/JR routes.
- Typical travel time: 20-40 min.
- Typical fare: JPY 260-500.

&emsp;&emsp;**Best season**

- Spring and autumn for outdoor historical sites.
