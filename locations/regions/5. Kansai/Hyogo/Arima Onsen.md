**Arima Onsen**

Arima Onsen is one of Kansai's best historic hot spring towns and an easy retreat from Kobe/Osaka.

&emsp;&emsp;**Top places**

- Kin no Yu and Gin no Yu bath areas
- Onsen streets and local sweets
- Ropeway and hill viewpoints (optional)

&emsp;&emsp;**Logistics**

- Access from Kobe via train + cable/bus or direct bus options.
- Typical fare range: JPY 700-2,000.

&emsp;&emsp;**Best season**

- Year-round for onsen.
- Autumn and winter are especially atmospheric.
