**Kobe**

Kobe is a port city with hillside views, Chinatown, and stylish waterfront districts.

&emsp;&emsp;**Top places**

- Harborland and Meriken Park
- Kitano historic district
- Nankinmachi (Chinatown)

&emsp;&emsp;**Logistics**

- Access from Osaka by JR/Hankyu/Hanshin.
- Typical travel time: 25-45 min.
- Typical fare: JPY 320-600.

&emsp;&emsp;**Best season**

- Spring and autumn for city and mountain-side walks.
