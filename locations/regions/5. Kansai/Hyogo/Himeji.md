**Himeji**

Himeji is home to Japan's most famous original castle and classic surrounding gardens.

&emsp;&emsp;**Top places**

- Himeji Castle
- Koko-en Garden
- Castle-town shopping streets

&emsp;&emsp;**Logistics**

- Access from Osaka/Kyoto by JR Special Rapid or Shinkansen.
- Typical one-way fare: JPY 1,500-5,500 depending on train type.

&emsp;&emsp;**Best season**

- Spring and autumn for best castle scenery.
