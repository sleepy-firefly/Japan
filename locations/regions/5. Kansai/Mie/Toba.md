**Toba**

Toba is a coastal city known for bay views, seafood, and easy access to nearby islands.

&emsp;&emsp;**Top places**

- Toba Aquarium
- Mikimoto Pearl Island
- Coastal viewpoints and seafood market areas

&emsp;&emsp;**Logistics**

- Access from Ise by Kintetsu/JR.
- Typical travel time: 15-25 min.
- Typical fare: JPY 300-600.

&emsp;&emsp;**Best season**

- Spring to autumn for coastal routes.
