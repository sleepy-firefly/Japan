**Ise**

Ise is one of Japan's most important spiritual destinations, centered on Ise Jingu and old pilgrimage streets.

&emsp;&emsp;**Top places**

- Ise Jingu (Naiku/Geku)
- Okage Yokocho and Oharai-machi
- Isuzugawa riverside area

&emsp;&emsp;**Logistics**

- Access from Nagoya/Osaka by Kintetsu or JR routes.
- Typical one-way fare range: JPY 2,000-6,000.

&emsp;&emsp;**Best season**

- Spring and autumn for shrine walks.
