**Shirahama**

Shirahama is a coastal resort town in Wakayama known for beaches, cliffs, and hot springs.

&emsp;&emsp;**Top places**

- Shirarahama Beach
- Sandanbeki and Senjojiki coastal formations
- Onsen stays and seafood areas

&emsp;&emsp;**Logistics**

- Access from Osaka: JR Kuroshio limited express.
- Typical travel time: 2.5-3 hours.
- Typical one-way fare: JPY 5,000-6,500.

&emsp;&emsp;**Best season**

- Summer for beaches.
- Spring/autumn for mixed sightseeing and onsen.
