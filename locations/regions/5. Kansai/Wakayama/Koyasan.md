**Koyasan**

Koyasan is a major sacred mountain center; for temple details see [Kongobu-ji](../../../../temples/Kongobu-ji.md).

&emsp;&emsp;**Top places**

- Okunoin cemetery route
- Danjo Garan complex
- Kongobu-ji Temple

&emsp;&emsp;**Logistics**

- Access from Osaka (Namba): Nankai Koya Line + cable car + bus.
- Typical one-way fare range: JPY 1,800-3,500.

&emsp;&emsp;**Best season**

- Spring and autumn for walking comfort.
- Winter for quieter temple-stay atmosphere.
