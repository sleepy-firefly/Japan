**Kumano Kodo Trail**

Kumano Kodo is a historic pilgrimage network through mountains, shrines, and rural villages in Wakayama.

&emsp;&emsp;**Suggested order (2-4 days)**

- Kii-Tanabe (trail entry point)
- Takijiri-oji to Chikatsuyu or Yunomine Onsen section
- Hongu Taisha area
- Optional Nachi Taisha and Nachi Falls extension

&emsp;&emsp;**Practical info**

- Access: JR Limited Express Kuroshio to Kii-Tanabe or Shingu, then local buses.
- Typical travel time from Osaka: about 2.5-4.5 hours depending on section.
- Typical one-way fare from Osaka area: JPY 4,000-8,000.

&emsp;&emsp;**Where to stay**

- Kii-Tanabe for first night logistics.
- Yunomine Onsen/Kawayu Onsen for trail-based overnight stays.

&emsp;&emsp;**Best season/month**

- March-May and October-November for best hiking weather.
- Summer is greener but hotter, more humid, and wetter.
