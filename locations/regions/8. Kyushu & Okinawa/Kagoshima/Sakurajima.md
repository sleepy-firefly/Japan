**Sakurajima**

Sakurajima is an active volcano island reached by short ferry from Kagoshima.

&emsp;&emsp;**Top places**

- Yunohira Observatory
- Lava trails and viewpoints
- Footbath/coastal park zones

&emsp;&emsp;**Logistics**

- Access: frequent ferry from Kagoshima Port.
- Ferry is short and inexpensive; local buses cover island viewpoints.

&emsp;&emsp;**Best season**

- Spring and autumn for clearer views and comfort.
- Check volcanic activity advisories before visiting.
