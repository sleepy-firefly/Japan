**Kagoshima City**

Kagoshima City offers bayfront views, volcanic scenery, and strong local food culture.

&emsp;&emsp;**Top places**

- Sengan-en Garden
- Waterfront viewpoints
- Local food districts

&emsp;&emsp;**Logistics**

- Access from Fukuoka by Kyushu Shinkansen.
- Typical one-way fare range: JPY 10,000-11,500.

&emsp;&emsp;**Best season**

- Spring and autumn for city and bay walks.
