**Yufuin**

Yufuin is a scenic onsen town with lake views, boutique streets, and mountain backdrops.

&emsp;&emsp;**Top places**

- Yunotsubo Street
- Lake Kinrin
- Onsen ryokan areas

&emsp;&emsp;**Logistics**

- Access from Beppu/Fukuoka by train or bus.
- Typical one-way fare range: JPY 1,000-5,500 depending on route.

&emsp;&emsp;**Best season**

- Spring and autumn for scenery.
- Winter for quiet onsen stays.
