**Beppu**

Beppu is one of Japan's biggest onsen cities with diverse hot spring experiences.

&emsp;&emsp;**Top places**

- Beppu Hells (Jigoku) route
- Onsen steam districts
- Seaside and market areas

&emsp;&emsp;**Logistics**

- Access from Fukuoka by JR limited express.
- Typical one-way fare range: JPY 4,500-7,000.

&emsp;&emsp;**Best season**

- Year-round onsen destination.
- Winter and autumn are especially pleasant.
