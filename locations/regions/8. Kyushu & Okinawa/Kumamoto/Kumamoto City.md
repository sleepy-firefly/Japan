**Kumamoto City**

Kumamoto City is known for castle reconstruction areas, gardens, and access to Aso region.

&emsp;&emsp;**Top places**

- Kumamoto Castle area
- Suizenji Jojuen Garden
- Central arcade and food streets

&emsp;&emsp;**Logistics**

- Access from Fukuoka by Kyushu Shinkansen.
- Typical one-way fare: JPY 5,000-6,000.

&emsp;&emsp;**Best season**

- Spring and autumn for city exploration.
