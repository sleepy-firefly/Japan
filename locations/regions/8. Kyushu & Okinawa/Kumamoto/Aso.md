**Aso**

Aso is a volcanic highland area with vast caldera landscapes and scenic grasslands.

&emsp;&emsp;**Top places**

- Kusasenri grasslands
- Aso volcano viewpoints (access-dependent)
- Onsen and rural stays

&emsp;&emsp;**Logistics**

- Access from Kumamoto by JR/bus/car.
- Conditions can change due to volcanic activity.

&emsp;&emsp;**Best season**

- May-October for greener landscapes.
- Autumn for clearer weather and cooler hikes.
