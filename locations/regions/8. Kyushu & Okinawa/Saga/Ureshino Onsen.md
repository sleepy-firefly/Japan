**Ureshino Onsen**

Ureshino Onsen is a historic hot spring town known for silky water and tea culture.

&emsp;&emsp;**Top places**

- Public and ryokan bathhouses
- Ureshino tea shops
- River walk areas

&emsp;&emsp;**Logistics**

- Access via Takeo Onsen area then local bus/car transfer.
- Final segment times vary by schedule.

&emsp;&emsp;**Best season**

- Year-round onsen destination.
- Autumn and winter are especially relaxing.
