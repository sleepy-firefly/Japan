**Karatsu**

Karatsu is a coastal castle town with pottery culture and a traditional festival history.

&emsp;&emsp;**Top places**

- Karatsu Castle
- Old merchant streets
- Coastal viewpoints

&emsp;&emsp;**Logistics**

- Access from Fukuoka by JR Chikuhi/Karatsu lines.
- Typical one-way fare range: JPY 700-1,300.

&emsp;&emsp;**Best season**

- Spring and autumn for mixed city/coast walking.
