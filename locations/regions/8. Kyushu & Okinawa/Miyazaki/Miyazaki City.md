**Miyazaki City**

Miyazaki City offers coastal parks, shrine sites, and a warmer southern climate.

&emsp;&emsp;**Top places**

- Aoshima coastal area
- Miyazaki Shrine
- Seaside parks and cafes

&emsp;&emsp;**Logistics**

- Access from Fukuoka/Kagoshima by train, bus, or domestic flight.
- Travel times vary significantly by mode.

&emsp;&emsp;**Best season**

- October-November and spring for comfortable weather.
