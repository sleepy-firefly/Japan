**Takachiho**

Takachiho is a mountain-gorge destination linked to mythology, waterfalls, and shrine culture.

&emsp;&emsp;**Top places**

- Takachiho Gorge boat/walk area
- Amanoiwato Shrine sites
- Kagura dance performances (schedule-based)

&emsp;&emsp;**Logistics**

- Access usually requires highway bus/car from Miyazaki/Kumamoto.
- Transfer planning is important due to limited frequency.

&emsp;&emsp;**Best season**

- Spring and autumn for hiking and gorge views.
