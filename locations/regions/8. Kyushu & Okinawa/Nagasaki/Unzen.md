**Unzen**

Unzen is a mountain onsen area known for volcanic steam fields and cooler highland climate.

&emsp;&emsp;**Top places**

- Unzen Jigoku steam fields
- Onsen hotels
- Mt. Unzen nature areas

&emsp;&emsp;**Logistics**

- Access usually via Isahaya and bus transfer.
- Transfer planning is important due to lower bus frequency.

&emsp;&emsp;**Best season**

- Spring and autumn for hikes and onsen comfort.
