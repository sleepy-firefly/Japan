**Nagasaki City**

Nagasaki offers layered history, harbor views, hillside neighborhoods, and strong Chinese-European cultural influences.

&emsp;&emsp;**Suggested order (1-2 days)**

- Peace Park and Atomic Bomb Museum
- Dejima and waterfront district
- Glover Garden and Oura Church area
- Night view from Mt. Inasa

&emsp;&emsp;**Logistics**

- Access from Fukuoka (Hakata): Limited Express relay + Nishi-Kyushu Shinkansen or highway bus.
- Typical travel time: about 1.5-2.5 hours.
- Typical one-way fare: JPY 3,000-8,000 depending on mode.

&emsp;&emsp;**Where to stay**

- Around Nagasaki Station or Chinatown area.

&emsp;&emsp;**Best season**

- March-May and October-November for mild weather and city walks.
