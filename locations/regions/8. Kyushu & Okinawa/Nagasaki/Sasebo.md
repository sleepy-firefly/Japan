**Sasebo**

Sasebo is a port city known for harbor cruises, island scenery, and naval-town history.

&emsp;&emsp;**Top places**

- Kujukushima cruise area
- Harbor viewpoints
- Local burger and seafood routes

&emsp;&emsp;**Logistics**

- Access from Nagasaki/Fukuoka by JR limited express routes.
- Fare and time vary by route choice.

&emsp;&emsp;**Best season**

- Spring and autumn for coastal sightseeing.
