**Hakata (Fukuoka District)**

Hakata is the transport and food heart of Fukuoka, known for station complexes and yatai culture.

&emsp;&emsp;**Highlights**

- Hakata Station area
- Canal City side routes
- Evening yatai food stalls nearby

&emsp;&emsp;**Best season**

- Year-round; best comfort in spring and autumn.
