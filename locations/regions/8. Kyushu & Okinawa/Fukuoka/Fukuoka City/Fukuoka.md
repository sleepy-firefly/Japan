**Fukuoka City**

Fukuoka City is a major Kyushu hub with food culture, bayside parks, and efficient transit.

&emsp;&emsp;**Suggested order (1-2 days)**

- Hakata old/new mix and temple side streets (see [Temple Index](../../../../temples/Temple%20Index.md))
- Tenjin shopping and nightlife
- Ohori Park and seaside area

&emsp;&emsp;**Logistics**

- Main transport: Fukuoka City Subway + Nishitetsu/JR links.
- Typical local fare: JPY 210-330.

&emsp;&emsp;**Best season**

- Spring and autumn for comfortable urban exploration.
