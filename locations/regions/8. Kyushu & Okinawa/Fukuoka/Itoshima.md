**Itoshima**

Itoshima is a coastal area west of Fukuoka known for beaches, cafes, and sunset viewpoints.

&emsp;&emsp;**Top places**

- Futamigaura coast and torii gate
- Beach cafes and local farms
- Coastal drive/photo spots

&emsp;&emsp;**Logistics**

- Access from Hakata/Tenjin area by JR Chikuhi Line + local bus/taxi.
- Typical one-way fare range: JPY 600-1,500.

&emsp;&emsp;**Best season**

- Spring to autumn for coastal sightseeing.
