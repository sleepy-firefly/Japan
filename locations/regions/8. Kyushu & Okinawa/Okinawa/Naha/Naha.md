**Naha**

Naha is Okinawa's capital with castle history, markets, and easy island-hopping access.

&emsp;&emsp;**Suggested order (1-2 days)**

- Shurijo area (reconstruction context)
- Kokusai Dori and Makishi market
- Port-side evening walks

&emsp;&emsp;**Logistics**

- Access from mainland by domestic flights.
- City transport: Yui Rail + buses/taxis.

&emsp;&emsp;**Best season**

- November-April for milder weather.
- Summer is beach-ready but hotter and more humid.
