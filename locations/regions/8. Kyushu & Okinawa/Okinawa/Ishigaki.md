**Ishigaki**

Ishigaki is a southern island destination known for beaches, snorkeling, and tropical scenery.

&emsp;&emsp;**Top places**

- Kabira Bay viewpoints
- Beach and marine activity areas
- Local market and island food streets

&emsp;&emsp;**Logistics**

- Access by domestic flight from Naha/mainland hubs.
- Local buses are available but slower than rental transport.

&emsp;&emsp;**Best season/month**

- April-October for beach conditions.
- Shoulder months are often less humid.
