**Osaka Science Museum (Nakanoshima, Osaka)**

Osaka Science Museum is a practical Kansai science stop with astronomy-focused exhibits and a planetarium program.

It is easy to combine with central Osaka sightseeing and riverfront routes.

<img src="../images/osaka_science_museum.jpg" alt="Osaka Science Museum and planetarium" style="width: 80%; height: auto;" />

&emsp;&emsp;**Best season/month**

- Year-round indoor destination.

&emsp;&emsp;**Practical note**

- Check planetarium session times before arrival to avoid long waits.
