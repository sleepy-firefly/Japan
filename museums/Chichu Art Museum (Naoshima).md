**Chichu Art Museum (Naoshima, Kagawa)**

Chichu Art Museum is one of Japan's most renowned contemporary art museums and a centerpiece of Naoshima art travel.

Reservations are often needed, and ferry timing affects day planning.

<img src="../images/chichu_art_museum.jpg" alt="Chichu Art Museum on Naoshima" style="width: 80%; height: auto;" />

&emsp;&emsp;**Best season/month**

- Spring and autumn for island walking and cycling conditions.

&emsp;&emsp;**Practical note**

- Reserve museum time slots before booking ferry-day itinerary.
