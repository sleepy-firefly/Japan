**JAXA Tsukuba Space Center (Tsukuba, Ibaraki)**

JAXA Tsukuba Space Center is one of the best places in Japan to learn about the country's real space program and astronaut training context.

It is a strong day trip from Tokyo for travelers specifically interested in rockets, satellites, and mission operations.

&emsp;&emsp;**Best season/month**

- Year-round; spring and autumn are most comfortable for the full campus visit.

&emsp;&emsp;**Practical note**

- Confirm guided tour availability and entry rules in advance; access policy can change.
