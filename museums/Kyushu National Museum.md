**Kyushu National Museum (Dazaifu, Fukuoka Prefecture)**

Kyushu National Museum is one of Japan's major national museums and a top cultural stop in Dazaifu.

It pairs well with Dazaifu Tenmangu and local shopping streets.

<img src="../images/kyushu_national_museum.jpg" alt="Kyushu National Museum in Dazaifu" style="width: 80%; height: auto;" />

&emsp;&emsp;**Best season/month**

- Year-round; especially good during hot or rainy weather.

&emsp;&emsp;**Practical note**

- Combine with shrine visit and afternoon museum session.
