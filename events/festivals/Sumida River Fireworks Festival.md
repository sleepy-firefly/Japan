**Sumida River Fireworks Festival**

The Sumida River Fireworks Festival (Sumidagawa Hanabi Taikai) is one of Tokyo's most famous summer events and is usually held in late July. Thousands of fireworks are launched over the Sumida River, creating a spectacular view of the night sky.

Many people gather along the river from early evening to reserve a good spot. You will often see visitors wearing yukata, and the festival atmosphere includes food stalls, traditional snacks, and lively crowds.

The best-known viewing areas are around Asakusa and near Tokyo Skytree, where the fireworks reflect over the river and city skyline. If you plan to attend, arriving early is essential because the area gets very crowded.
