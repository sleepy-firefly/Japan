**Sawara Grand Festival**

The Sawara Grand Festival is a traditional float festival in Sawara (Katori, Chiba), typically held in summer and autumn editions. Large decorated floats, historic streets, and festival music create a strong Edo-period atmosphere.

The event is often highlighted for the contrast between preserved canal-town scenery and dynamic parade movement. It is a strong cultural stop for travelers interested in local traditions outside central Tokyo.

&emsp;&emsp;**Practical info**

- Typical timing: July (summer edition) and October (autumn edition), exact dates vary.
- Main area: historic streets around Sawara canal district.
- Best strategy: stay overnight in Sawara or Narita area during festival weekends.
