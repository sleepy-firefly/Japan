**Comiket (Comic Market, Tokyo)**

Comiket is Japan's largest fan-created manga/anime convention and one of the biggest otaku events in the world.

It is held twice a year in Tokyo and is known for doujinshi, cosplay, fan art culture, and large-scale community participation.

- Winter edition (December)

- Expect very high attendance and long entry queues; arrive early and hydrate.
