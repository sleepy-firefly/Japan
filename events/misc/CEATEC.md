**CEATEC (Chiba, Makuhari Messe)**

CEATEC is one of Japan's leading annual technology expos, covering digital innovation, electronics, AI, smart infrastructure, and mobility.

It is a strong choice for travelers interested in business-facing and consumer-facing technology trends.

&emsp;&emsp;**Typical timing**

- Usually held in October (annual)

- Program mixes business sessions and public exhibition areas; pre-check event map.
